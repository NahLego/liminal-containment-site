// ABOUT THE GAME

LIMINAL CONTAINMENT is an independent survival horror game set at the intersection of SCP Foundation lore and Backrooms anomalous space theory.

You play as D-XXXX — a D-Class subject assigned to an observational programme of unspecified duration. Equipped with a scanner, a camera, and very little else, you are sent into a clipping site from which no MTF unit has returned.

Keep the camera running. Keep moving.
